The Science of Supplements

Why Creatine?

Creatine monohydrate is one of the most researched supplements. It has been proven to increase strength, power, and speed up recovery. The standard daily dose is 3–5 g.

Protein After Training

Consuming 20–30 g of high-quality protein within 1 hour after training supports muscle protein synthesis and improves recovery.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

EPA and DHA support heart, brain, and joint health. Recommendation: 1–2 g of combined EPA+DHA daily.

Multivitamins

They can be helpful when the diet lacks variety or when needs are increased (stress, intense training, lack of sleep).
