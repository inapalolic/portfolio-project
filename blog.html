How to Build a Supplement Routine

Published: September 25, 2025

Combining supplements depends on your goal. The most common basic stack includes:

  • Protein after training
  • Creatine daily (3–5 g)
  • Omega-3 with a meal
  • Multivitamin as needed

Multivitamins – When Do They Make Sense?

Published: September 20, 2025

Multivitamins are useful if:

  • Your diet is repetitive
  • You have increased needs (stress, intense training, illness)
  • You don’t manage to consume enough fruits and vegetables

Recommendation: choose comprehensive multivitamins with clearly stated dosages, without unnecessary additives.
